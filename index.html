TuneTap

Tap a physical tag → the right Spotify playlist starts playing. No menus, no searching.

  1. 1Install the TuneTap app and sign in with Spotify Premium.
  2. 2Write a playlist to a blank NFC tag from inside the app.
  3. 3Tap your phone to the tag — playback begins instantly.
